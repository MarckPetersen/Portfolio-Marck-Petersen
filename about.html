Meet Marck Petersen

Hi there! I'm Marck Petersen, a seasoned Quality Assurance wizard who has transitioned to developing innovative solutions using tools like ChatGPT, Gherkin syntax, and GitHub to design and describe software without writing traditional code. Think of me as the Sherlock Holmes of QA—but instead of solving crimes, I solve bugs and inefficiencies. With over 30 years of experience in Quality Assurance and Test Management, I've danced my way through waterfall and agile projects, ensuring software perfection across platforms and industries.

Turning chaos into order while making sure no bug gets past my watchful eyes.
